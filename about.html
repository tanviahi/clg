About Us

Our Legacy & Vision

Founded by the visionary Late Dr. Shivajirao S. Jhondele, our institutions have been at the forefront of technical education in Maharashtra for decades. With a commitment to excellence and innovation, we strive to create an environment that fosters intellectual growth and professional development.

Our campuses across Thane district - Dombivli, Asangaon, and Ambernath - are equipped with modern infrastructure, experienced faculty, and industry connections to provide comprehensive technical education.

We believe in holistic development, combining academic rigor with extracurricular activities to prepare students for successful careers in engineering and management.

Our Institutions:

  • SSJP College of Engineering - Dombivli
  • SSJP Polytechnic - Asangaon
  • SSJP College of Engineering & Technology - Ambernath

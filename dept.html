Departments

Computer Engineering

The Computer Engineering department offers comprehensive programs in software development, data structures, algorithms, and computer networks. Our labs are equipped with the latest hardware and software tools.

Programs Offered: B.E. Computer Engineering, M.E. Computer Engineering

Information Technology

The IT department focuses on emerging technologies like cloud computing, cybersecurity, data science, and web technologies. We emphasize practical skills alongside theoretical knowledge.

Programs Offered: B.E. Information Technology, M.E. Information Technology

Mechanical Engineering

Our Mechanical Engineering department offers training in design, manufacturing, thermal sciences, and robotics. We have advanced workshops and CAD/CAM labs for hands-on experience.

Programs Offered: B.E. Mechanical Engineering, M.E. Mechanical Engineering

Electrical Engineering

The Electrical Engineering department covers power systems, control systems, electronics, and renewable energy technologies. Our labs include high-voltage equipment and automation systems.

Programs Offered: B.E. Electrical Engineering, M.E. Electrical Engineering

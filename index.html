About Shivajirao S. Jondhale

Shivajirao S. Jhondele refers to several educational institutions in the Thane district, Maharashtra, founded by the Jhondele family, primarily offering technical education in engineering and management.

Our institutions include SSJP College of Engineering (Dombivli), SSJP Polytechnic (Asangaon), and SSJP College of Engineering & Technology (Ambernath), focusing on AICTE/MSBTE-affiliated programs.

We aim to provide quality education with infrastructure and placement outcomes across our campuses, nurturing future leaders in technology and management.

About Us

Our Legacy & Vision

Founded by the visionary Late Dr. Shivajirao S. Jhondele, our institutions have been at the forefront of technical education in Maharashtra for decades. With a commitment to excellence and innovation, we strive to create an environment that fosters intellectual growth and professional development.

Our campuses across Thane district - Dombivli, Asangaon, and Ambernath - are equipped with modern infrastructure, experienced faculty, and industry connections to provide comprehensive technical education.

We believe in holistic development, combining academic rigor with extracurricular activities to prepare students for successful careers in engineering and management.

Our Institutions:

  • SSJP College of Engineering - Dombivli
  • SSJP Polytechnic - Asangaon
  • SSJP College of Engineering & Technology - Ambernath

Departments

Computer Engineering

The Computer Engineering department offers comprehensive programs in software development, data structures, algorithms, and computer networks. Our labs are equipped with the latest hardware and software tools.

Programs Offered: B.E. Computer Engineering, M.E. Computer Engineering

Information Technology

The IT department focuses on emerging technologies like cloud computing, cybersecurity, data science, and web technologies. We emphasize practical skills alongside theoretical knowledge.

Programs Offered: B.E. Information Technology, M.E. Information Technology

Mechanical Engineering

Our Mechanical Engineering department offers training in design, manufacturing, thermal sciences, and robotics. We have advanced workshops and CAD/CAM labs for hands-on experience.

Programs Offered: B.E. Mechanical Engineering, M.E. Mechanical Engineering

Electrical Engineering

The Electrical Engineering department covers power systems, control systems, electronics, and renewable energy technologies. Our labs include high-voltage equipment and automation systems.

Programs Offered: B.E. Electrical Engineering, M.E. Electrical Engineering

Courses Offered

Computer Technology

Diploma in Computer Technology

Duration: 3 Years

Eligibility: 10th Pass

Intake: 60 Seats

This course provides comprehensive knowledge in programming, software development, database management, and networking. Students learn languages like Java, Python, C++, and web technologies.

Key Subjects:

  • Programming in C & C++
  • Web Development
  • Database Management
  • Computer Networks
  • Software Engineering
Information Technology

Diploma in Information Technology

Duration: 3 Years

Eligibility: 10th Pass

Intake: 60 Seats

Key Subjects:

  • Cloud Computing

  • Mobile App Development
  • IT Project Management
Mechanical Engineering

Duration: 3 Years
